Why this feels different

  • The board is organized around a real day, not an endless list.
  • The app recommends what fits now instead of asking you to decide from scratch.
  • Focus tasks, admin work, and personal items can coexist without feeling messy.
  • Your data is stored by your own Node server on your own machine.
